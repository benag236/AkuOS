Auto-Categorization Rules

Teach the app how to classify transactions automatically. Higher-priority and more specific rules win first, then merchant memory, then built-in rules, then review fallback.

Priority decides who wins A higher priority number beats a lower one. If two rules are close, exact and starts-with matches still outrank broader contains matches.
Direction prevents bad matches Use `debit` for spending, `credit` for deposits or refunds, and `any` only when the rule truly applies both ways.
Merchant Memory works with rules Correcting imports and transactions teaches the app. You can manage remembered merchants directly on the Merchant Memory page.
